Matthias Stange
Born in 1985 and raised in Stuttgart, he studied history and rhetoric in Tübingen and Berlin, then worked as a scientific trainee for the Berlin Wall Foundation.
He now writes about the Berlin remembrance culture of the 1980s and works as a freelance event photographer for various memorial sites and public institutions. 
His focus lies on documenting the city's transformation, the experiences of its residents and public remembrance practices.
